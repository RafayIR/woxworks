HEADS

starting at €2000

Our standard heads are constructed using lightweight foam and feature: 3D printed follow-me eyes, sublimated mesh eyes, plastic teeth, minky tongue and nose, and static jaw.

​Each head is also fully lined to ensure no exposed foam and comes with hidden vents for proper ventilation. We line the neck partially (only where it touches the skin) to avoid folding the fur.

It's essential to keep in mind that the more add-ons you choose, the heavier the head will become. We do our best to maintain a lightweight design.​

However, there are certain features we do not offer, such as moving jaws or electronics, resin bases, realistic eyes, or airbrushing.

Eyes

Mesh 1

Mesh type 1 provides significantly improved visibility while sacrificing some finer details. It's an excellent choice for those seeking clear visibility, especially for gradients, simple designs, or individuals planning to engage in active suiting. From a distance, the holes in the mesh become practically invisible.

Mesh 2

Mesh type 2 features much finer spacing, which may result in slightly darker or more blurry vision. However, this mesh is perfect for adding intricate details, making it an excellent choice for complex characters. Furthermore, the finer mesh allows for increased saturation since more of the surface can be printed on, allowing the colors to pop and enhancing the overall visual appeal.

Our eyes are crafted using sublimation printing onto PVC mesh, which enables us to achieve exceptional accuracy and intricate details in the eyes. These eyes are completely waterproof, and for an added layer of protection against scratches, they are coated with a clear spray. As the eyes are printed, it's important to note that the colors may not be 100% accurate. However, rest assured that we put in our best effort to match the colors as closely as possible. We conduct numerous tests before printing the final product to ensure the best results.

Plush teeth are made with soft muchi fabric and thread sculpted to make each individual tooth, they are sewn and glued to the mouth

toony style

Toony style handpaws are 4 finger handpaws with a thumb, easy to tell the difference from Left and Right.

feral style
